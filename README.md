# PAS Outcomes Calculator

A clinical calculator for predicting adverse outcomes in patients with suspected Placenta Accreta Spectrum (PAS).

## Outcomes Predicted
- ICU Admission
- Blood Transfusion
- Surgical Assistance Required
- Hysterectomy
- High Blood Loss (>2.6L)
- Extended Hospital Stay (>5 days)

## How to Use
1. Enter clinical information (prior C-sections, previa status, gestational age, SDI)
2. Select MRI findings (if MRI was performed)
3. Click "Calculate Risk Probabilities"
4. View predicted probabilities for each outcome

## Model Information
- Based on logistic regression models
- Developed from Johns Hopkins Hospital cohort
- AUC ranges from 0.718 to 0.865 depending on outcome

## Disclaimer
This calculator is for educational and research purposes only. Not a substitute for clinical judgment.
